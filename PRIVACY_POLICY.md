# Privacy Policy for Canvas LM

**Last Updated:** November 24, 2025  
**Effective Date:** November 24, 2025

## Introduction

Canvas LM ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services.

By using Canvas LM, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
We collect the following personal information:

- **Google Account Information**: When you sign in using Chrome's Identity API, we collect:
  - Email address
  - Display name
  - User ID (provided by Google)
  - Profile picture URL (if available)

### 2. Course Information
We collect and store:

- Canvas course IDs
- Course names
- Course enrollment records
- PDF documents and course materials you choose to upload
- Document metadata (filename, upload date, file size)

### 3. Usage Information
We automatically collect:

- Chat conversation history with the AI assistant
- Questions you ask and AI responses
- Timestamps of interactions
- Course access patterns
- Feature usage statistics

### 4. Technical Information
- Extension version
- Browser type and version
- Error logs and diagnostic information
- Cloud Function execution logs

### 5. Canvas Integration Data
When you use Canvas LM on Canvas pages:
- Canvas session cookies (used only to download PDFs with your permission)
- Canvas page URLs (to detect which course you're viewing)
- PDF download requests

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Primary Functions
1. **Authentication**: Verify your identity using Google sign-in
2. **Course Management**: Track your course enrollments and access
3. **AI Assistance**: Process your questions and provide answers based on course materials
4. **Document Storage**: Store and index your course PDFs for AI processing
5. **Conversation History**: Maintain your chat sessions for continuity

### Service Improvement
1. **Performance Monitoring**: Track usage to identify and fix issues
2. **Feature Development**: Understand how features are used to guide improvements
3. **Security**: Detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access

### Communications
1. **Service Updates**: Notify you of important changes or updates
2. **Support**: Respond to your inquiries and support requests

## How We Store Your Information

### Data Storage
- **Location**: All data is stored in Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) servers in Europe (europe-north1 - Finland)
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- **Access Control**: Only you and authorized Cloud Functions can access your data

### Data Retention
- **Course Materials**: Retained as long as you remain enrolled in the course
- **Chat History**: Retained indefinitely unless you delete it
- **User Profile**: Retained until you request deletion
- **Logs**: System logs retained for 30 days for debugging purposes

## Third-Party Services

Canvas LM integrates with the following third-party services:

### 1. Google Cloud Platform / Firebase
- **Purpose**: Data storage (Firestore), Cloud Functions, authentication
- **Data Shared**: All collected information
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 2. Google AI (Gemini API)
- **Purpose**: AI-powered question answering using course materials
- **Data Shared**: Your questions, course materials (PDFs), conversation context
- **Privacy Policy**: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms

- **Purpose**: Extension distribution and updates

- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Use your data for marketing purposes
- ❌ Share your course materials publicly
- ❌ Allow other users to access your private data

### We MAY Share Information:
- ✅ **With your course**: Course materials are stored in shared course databases that other enrolled students may access
- ✅ **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights
- ✅ **Service Providers**: With Google Cloud Platform to operate the service
- ✅ **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data**: View all information we have about you
2. **Delete Your Data**: Request deletion of your personal information
3. **Export Your Data**: Download your chat history and course data
4. **Correct Information**: Update incorrect personal information
5. **Opt-Out**: Stop using the service at any time

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- **Delete Chat History**: Use the delete button in the extension
- **Unenroll from Courses**: Remove enrollment through the extension
- **Delete Account**: Email us at rasmus.alpsten@gmail.com
- **Export Data**: Email us at rasmus.alpsten@gmail.com

### Data Deletion Process
When you request account deletion:
1. Your user profile is removed
2. Your enrollments are removed
3. Your chat history is deleted
4. Your personal information is anonymized in logs
5. Shared course materials remain (as they may be used by others)

**Note**: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Children's Privacy

Canvas LM is intended for use by students and educational institutions. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 without parental consent or school authorization.

If you are under 13, please:
- Obtain permission from your parent or guardian
- Use Canvas LM only through your educational institution
- Do not provide personal information without permission

If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13 without proper authorization, we will delete it promptly.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored in servers located in the European Union (Finland). By using Canvas LM, you consent to this transfer.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this privacy policy.

## Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

### Technical Safeguards
- ✅ HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
- ✅ Firestore security rules to prevent unauthorized access
- ✅ Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- ✅ API keys stored securely server-side only
- ✅ Regular security audits and updates

### Operational Safeguards
- ✅ Access controls and authentication
- ✅ Monitoring and logging for suspicious activity
- ✅ Incident response procedures
- ✅ Regular backups

**However**, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Cookies and Tracking

### Canvas Session Cookies
Canvas LM accesses Canvas session cookies **only** when you explicitly scan a course for PDFs. These cookies are:
- Used temporarily to authenticate PDF download requests
- Never stored permanently
- Never shared with third parties
- Immediately discarded after use

### Extension Storage
We use Chrome's local storage to:
- Remember your preferences
- Cache course information for performance
- Store session data temporarily

You can clear this data through Chrome's extension settings.
